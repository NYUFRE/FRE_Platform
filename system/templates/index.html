Manual Trading and Portfolio Management

This is a paper trading system where a user can perform manual trading and manage a personal trading portfolio.
Each registered user has initial fund of $10,000.

Portfolio

1) Shows current holdings, average cost of each holding, cash value, portfolio value, and unrealized PnL.

2) Each holding in the portfolio is calculated in real-time based on the latest sale price of the stock from IEX.

Stock Quote

1) Show real-time quotes from market data provider IEX.

2) While the market is open, the quote shows best bid and ask quotes as well as the latest sale price.

Buy/Sell Stock

1) Provides manual trading based on quotes.

2) Market orders will be traded at quoted bid and ask price.

3) Limit buy order will trade at quoted ask price.
    • If a buy order price is higher than ask price, the order will receive price improvement, i.e. filled at the ask price.
    • If a buy order price is lower than the ask price, the order will be rejected.

4) Limit Sell order will trade at quoted bid price.
    • If a sell order price is lower than bid price, the order will receive price improvement, i.e. filled at bid price.
    • If a sell order price is higher than the bid price, the order will be rejected.

5) Market orders and limit orders could be either fully filled and partially filled based on quote bid and ask size.
    • If an order quantity is equal to or less than corresponding quote size, the order will be fully filled.
    • Otherwise, the order will be partially filled.

Short Stock

1) Similar ability to short stock as brokerage account does.

2) Open a short position would add negative shares to portfolio and increase cash amount.

3) To close a short position simply use buy stock function to buy back.

4) Short position has few thresholds.
    • User cannot hold both long position and short position.
    • User required to hold enough cash to make a short position order.

History

Show the symbol, price, quantity, date, and time for every trade.
