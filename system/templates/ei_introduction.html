Earnings Impact Analysis

This module conduct the earnings impact analysis in sp500 stocks

1) Get earnings calendar from the Internet using packages.

2) Split earnings calendar according to eps surprise and group into three.

3) Get historical returns in the Internet.

4) Locate announcement day and fill 60 returns nearby into nparray.

5) Conduct the bootstrap analysis.
